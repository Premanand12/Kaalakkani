Download Noto Sans Tamil fonts from:
https://fonts.google.com/noto/specimen/Noto+Sans+Tamil

Required files (place in this folder):
  NotoSansTamil-Regular.ttf    (weight 400)
  NotoSansTamil-Medium.ttf     (weight 500)
  NotoSansTamil-SemiBold.ttf   (weight 600)
  NotoSansTamil-Bold.ttf       (weight 700)

These are bundled in the app - never fetched at runtime.
