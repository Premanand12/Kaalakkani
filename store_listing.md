English:
கaalakkani is a complete Tamil Panchangam app with zero compromise.

✓ No registration ever — open the app, see today's Panchangam immediately
✓ No ads on the Panchangam screen — ever
✓ Works completely offline — no internet needed for Panchangam data
✓ 10 years of historical data bundled — look up any date from 2015
✓ Smart Muhurtham Finder — find auspicious dates for weddings, housewarming, vehicle purchase
✓ Live gold rate + petrol price — so you never need to open another app
✓ Marriage Porutham — all 10 poruthams calculated instantly
✓ Festival animated banners — Diwali, Pongal, Karthigai with unique animations
✓ Elder Mode — 25% larger text for easy reading by senior family members
✓ Daily Thirukkural with meaning
✓ Moi (wedding gift) register — stored locally, never uploaded

Available in Tamil. Supports Vakiya and Drik calculation systems.
